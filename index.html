Philosophy — 01

Hair is a
language.

Your hair speaks before you do. It carries the record of every decision, every season, every chapter of your life — written in texture, density and shine.

Breakage after a difficult year. Thinning after a pregnancy. Dullness after months of poor sleep. None of this is coincidence. Your hair is a biological diary — precise, honest, and deeply personal.

Hebra was built to read that language — and to translate it into something actionable. Not to make you feel that something is wrong, but to give you the clarity to make it right.

The Six Pillars We Measure

Scalp Health

follicle density, sebum balance and inflammation signals from your root zone

Strength

cortex resilience, protein bond integrity and breakage risk along the shaft

Moisture

porosity patterns, hydration capacity and dryness markers from root to tip

Integrity

split ends, cuticle lift and surface damage — the cumulative record of your hair history

Vitality

growth cycle activity, shedding patterns and overall strand density over time

Ingredients

every product recommendation is matched to your diagnosis using clean, evidence-based actives

Philosophy — 03

Precision
care.

Generic routines produce generic results. Hebra gives you exactly what your hair needs — nothing more, nothing less.

Precision care means understanding that what works for one person actively harms another. Protein treatments that restore one hair type will make another brittle. The moisturiser that transforms dry hair will weigh down fine hair for days.

Hebra does not give you a shortlist of popular products. It gives you the specific intervention your hair requires right now — ranked by impact, filtered by what you already own, calibrated to the time you have available.

Diagnose

We analyse your photos and questionnaire to score five dimensions of hair health.

Prioritise

We identify your most critical deficiencies and rank interventions by expected impact.

Prescribe

We recommend only products you don't already own, matched to your exact hair type and goals.

Evolve

As your hair changes, your diagnosis changes. Repeat the scan any time to track your progress.
